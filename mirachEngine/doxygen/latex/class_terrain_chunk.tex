\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a sub-\/section of a larger terrain for LOD or streaming. 

Inherits from \doxylink{class_mesh_terrain}{Mesh\+Terrain} and extracts its data from a global heightmap. This allows terrain to be split into smaller pieces for performance and culling.
