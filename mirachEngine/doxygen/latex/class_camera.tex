\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A 3D camera that handles projection, view matrix generation, frustum culling, and orientation logic. 

Used in the Mirach engine to compute view and projection matrices, manage orientation using pitch and yaw, and determine whether objects are visible within the view frustum.
